About VG College

VG College is dedicated to providing high-quality education and fostering a supportive learning environment. Our programs are designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in their chosen fields. From cutting-edge technology courses like BSc CSIT to comprehensive BBA training, we prioritize excellence in education and student success.

Located in the heart of Biratnagar city, Mahendra Chowk, VG College offers a dynamic and engaging learning atmosphere. Our experienced faculty members are industry experts, bringing real-world insights into the classroom to enhance student learning. Additionally, we offer generous scholarships for deserving candidates, ensuring that opportunities for higher education are accessible to all.

VG MOU
MoUs for IT and Management at VG College

At VG College, we are committed to providing our students with opportunities for hands-on learning and real-world industry exposure through strategic partnerships with leading organizations. Our MoUs with top companies in IT and Management sectors ensure that our students receive cutting-edge training, internship opportunities, and practical experience that align with industry standards.

For our IT programs, VG College has established MoUs with several reputed organizations:

  • NDF (NUMA Digital Farm): This partnership focuses on digital innovation and technology training, providing students with access to workshops, internships, and real-time projects in digital farming and technology integration.
  • AIT (Academy for Innovation in Technology): Through this MoU, students can benefit from specialized training sessions, certification courses, and mentorship programs aimed at fostering innovation and entrepreneurship in the field of information technology.
  • IT Training Nepal: This collaboration ensures that students receive industry-oriented IT training and upskilling programs, equipping them with the latest skills required in the IT job market.
  • Prabidhi: VG College's partnership with Prabidhi offers students exposure to technology-driven projects and real-time problem-solving, preparing them for a successful career in software development, web technologies, and IT services.

For our Management programs, VG College has also formed valuable partnerships with notable organizations:

  • Makalu Television HD and Max TV: These MoUs allow management students to gain hands-on experience in the media industry through internships, studio visits, and opportunities to work on live broadcasting projects.
  • Belbari Housing: This collaboration offers students insights into the real estate industry, providing practical training in property management, housing projects, and client relations.
  • Paras Multispeciality Hospital Pvt. Ltd.: With this MoU, management students can explore healthcare management, hospital administration, and customer service roles through internships and workshops, enhancing their understanding of the healthcare sector.
These strategic partnerships with leading IT and management organizations not only enrich the educational experience at VG College but also prepare our students to thrive in the competitive job market.
