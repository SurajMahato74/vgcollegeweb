Bachelor of Business Administration

The BBA program at VG College, delivered in collaboration with RJU University, Nepal, offers a dynamic curriculum designed to prepare students for successful careers in the business world. Covering essential topics such as marketing, finance, human resource management, and entrepreneurship, the program emphasizes both foundational business principles and contemporary practices in a rapidly evolving global market. With a focus on cultivating innovative problem solvers and effective communicators, the BBA program prepares graduates for diverse roles in various sectors, including corporate management, consulting, and startups. Whether aspiring to lead teams, launch their own businesses, or drive organizational growth, students will emerge as well-rounded professionals ready to make impactful contributions in the business arena.
